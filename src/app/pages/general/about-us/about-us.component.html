Who We Are

Shall We Connect is a professional matchmaking platform that pairs people for live video conversations based on shared interests, industry, location, and goals. Whether you're looking for an instant connection or want to schedule a meeting at a convenient time, we make meaningful introductions effortless.

Our Mission

To break down barriers between professionals and create genuine human connections in a digital-first world. We believe every conversation has the potential to spark something great — a collaboration, a friendship, or the next big idea.

What We Offer

  • Instant Connect — jump into a live video call with a matched user right now
  • Scheduled Meetings — book a video call at a time that works for both of you
  • Smart Filters — match by age, gender, company, college, location and industry
  • In-app Video Calls — no third-party apps needed, everything runs in your browser
  • Feedback & Rounds — give feedback after each meeting and choose to connect again

Why Choose Us?

Real-Time Video

Peer-to-peer WebRTC calls mean zero latency and full privacy — no recording, no middleman.

Intelligent Matching

Our compatibility engine scores candidates on industry, location, timezone and preference.

Flexible Scheduling

Can't connect right now? Schedule a meeting and get a reminder when it's time to join.

Privacy First
